Xiangjie Sui is a Ph.D. candidate with the School of Information Management at Jiangxi University of Finance and Economics (JUFE). He received the B.E. and MASc. degrees from the JUFE in 2018 and 2021, respectively. Currently, he is a visiting student with the Department of Computer Science at City University (CityU) of Hong Kong, from 2023 to 2024. His research interests include visual signal processing and quality assessment,  virtual reality (VR) image representation and analysis.

## Perceptual Quality Assessment of 360$^\circ$ Images Based on Generative Scanpath Representation

**Xiangjie Sui**\*, Hanwei Zhu\*, Yuming Fang, Shiqi Wang, Zhou Wang

⭐ A novel generative scanpath representation for 360$^\circ$ images, featuring with 
(1) **adaptability** -- adapt to a predefined viewing condition which is characterized by a starting point and an exploration time;
(2) **modeling of multi-hypothesis users** -- provide a comprehensive global overview of dynamic perceptual experiences of multi-hypothesis users.

⭐ An efficient computational framework, with higher accuracy, greater robustness, and less computational complexity.

## ScanDMM: A Deep Markov Model of Scanpath Prediction for 360$^\circ$ Images

**Xiangjie Sui**, Yuming Fang, Hanwei Zhu, Shiqi Wang, Zhou Wang

⭐ Time-dependent visual attention modeling in 360$^\circ$ scenes. We model the mechanism of visual working memory by maintaining and updating the visual state in the Markov chain.

⭐ Make predictions under a predefined viewing condition, which is characterized by a starting point and an exploration time.

⭐ Equipped with crucial capabilities, i.e., high accuracy, efficiency and generalizability, for real-world applications.

[**[Paper]**](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content/CVPR2023/papers/Sui_ScanDMM_A_Deep_Markov_Model_of_Scanpath_Prediction_for_360deg_CVPR_2023_paper.pdf)[**[Code]**](https://github.com/xiangjieSui/ScanDMM)
