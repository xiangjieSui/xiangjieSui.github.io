Biography


Xiangjie Sui is an assistant professor at Faculty of Data Science of City University of Macau. He received the B.E., M.A.Sc. And Ph.D. degrees from the Jiangxi University of Finance and Economics, Nanchang, China, in 2018, 2021, and 2024 respectively (Supervisor: Prof. Yuming Fang). He was a Visiting Student with the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong SAR, China, from 2023 to 2024. (Supervisor: Prof. Shiqi Wang). His research interests include visual signal processing and quality assessment, virtual reality (VR) image representation and analysis.
