Biography


Xiangjie Sui is a Ph.D. candidate with the School of Information Management at Jiangxi University of Finance and Economics (JUFE), under the supervision of Prof. Yuming Fang. He received the B.E. and MASc. degrees from the JUFE in 2018 and 2021, respectively. Currently, he is a visiting student with the Department of Computer Science at City University (CityU) of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Prof. Shiqi Wang. His research interests include visual signal processing and quality assessment, virtual reality (VR) image representation and analysis.
